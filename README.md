I am a **Full-Stack Software Developer** based in Granada, Spain. My professional journey is built on a foundation of **Tourism and Innovation**, having lived and worked in the UK, Slovakia, and various cities in Spain. 🌍

Currently, I am pivoting my career toward the technology sector while working at **Muziker** in International Customer Service. Managing multiple global markets simultaneously has sharpened my ability to solve complex problems under pressure and communicate across cultures—skills I now apply to writing clean, efficient code.

- 🛠️ **The Bootcamp:** I'm completing a Full-Stack program at **[4Geeks Academy](https://github.com/4geeksacademy)**, mastering **Frontend (React, JS)** and **Backend (Python, Flask, SQL)** development.
- 🎯 **The Mission:** My goal is to transition into a **Software Developer** or **Technical Support Engineer** role where I can leverage my international experience to build and maintain high-quality digital products.
- 🏗️ **Current Projects:** Developing a **Star Wars Blog API** using **Flask and SQLAlchemy** to manage relational data, and refining my React skills through interactive projects.
